Services

Dialed bikes. Dangerous confidence.

Dom brings tools, instinct, and rider IQ. From a flat repair to jump confidence, the goal is simple: make the bike feel locked in and make the rider feel powerful without losing control.

Mobile Bike Repair

On-site tune-ups, adjustments, basic maintenance, and ride-readiness checks.

Trail Prep

Pre-ride inspections for mountain routes, trail days, longer rides, and first rides back.

Flat Repair

Tube and tire support, pressure checks, and simple troubleshooting before the next ride.

Brake + Gear Adjustments

Brake feel, shifting performance, cable tension, and control-point checks.

Bike Coaching

One-on-one help for body position, braking, climbing, cornering, handling, jumping, and control.

Ride Support

Ride planning, gear checks, route prep, confidence, and personal progression goals.
