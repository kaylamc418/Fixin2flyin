Fixin’ 2 Flyin’

**Fixin’ 2 Flyin’** is a mobile bike repair and bike coaching website for Dom. The site is built around mobile bicycle repair, tune-ups, trail prep, ride support, coaching, Lumi, and a visible tribute to Dom’s mama.

## Live Site

https://domproject.kayamc418.workers.dev/

## Website Purpose

This site is designed to show:

* Mobile bike repair services
* Bike coaching and rider confidence support
* Trail prep and ride-readiness checks
* Dom’s bike life, mountain rides, and gallery images
* Lumi as part of the story
* A tribute section for Dom’s mama
* A simple contact/booking area
